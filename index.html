The Somerville Community Path

Hike, bike, rollerblade, birdwatch, or even ski on on these proposed paths in Massachusetts

Community Paths

The Somerville Community Path is a critical link in the network of paths and greenways in eastern Massachusetts.

The path does or will connect to the following community paths.

West

  • Linear Path from Davis Square to Alewife junction
  • Fitchburg Cutoff
  • Belmont
  • Mass Central Rail Trail
  • Northampton

Northwest

  • Minuteman Bikeway from Alewife junction
  • Concord and Billerica from Bedford

North (then East)

  • Alewife Brook
  • Mystic River paths
  • Bike to the Sea
  • Boston Harbor

Southwest

  • Watertown Branch to the Charles River

East

  • Grand Junction trail in Cambridge
  • NorthPoint to the Charles River parks
  • Charles River paths
